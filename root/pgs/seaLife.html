Irishwrecks Online

This award winning site is the on-going result of many years of gathering information on the dived wrecks around the whole of the Irish Coast.
Wreck site information is in a “potted” form
(as most divers only require the bare bones with regards to historical details).

How to navigate:

  • Wrecks of Ireland

    Dive sites listed by County and nearby town.

  • Search Wrecks

    Use an advance search to find a wreck to fit your ability and experience.

  • Anatomy of a Ship

    Illustrations of different ship types.

  • Dive in

    Great websites to aid in planning a dive or a visit to this wonderful island!

  • Sea Life

    Animals to look for underwater and near the shore.

  • Dive Forum

    Shared diving experiences.

  • Top Rated Wrecks

    Best dive locations.

  • Bibliography

  • Terminology

    Where landlubbers learn the lingo.

  • Contact us

    We look forward to hearing from you!
